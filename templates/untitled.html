PAINTING

About the event

Anyone can learn how to paint! In this course, students of all skill levels will learn how to render the objects they see with accuracy and confidence while developing their own artistic expression. We will begin by learning how to draw objects from life using a block-in-method. We will discuss shading, proportion, value, color, and composition.
